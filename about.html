SO FAR

For me what led me down the path of AI and machine learning was my internship at Epson. My mentor was interested in machine learning and wanted me to create a prototype chat bot using only open source resources. We worked in collaboration and his insight filled in some of the gaps in my own research.

This interest had me take a machine learning class the following semester learning more about what happens behind the open source models I was using. Since I have graduated I have reflected in what I want to pursue and came back to Huggingface - a huge source of knowledge for my past projects. Huggingface coupled with online influences like AI Warehouse sparked my pursuit in understanding reinforcement learning and seeking out opportunities where I can learn more about machine learning in a professional environment. One of my lastest achievements is my completed certification in reinforcement learning by Huggingface as I've recognized it as a start for what I can do.
